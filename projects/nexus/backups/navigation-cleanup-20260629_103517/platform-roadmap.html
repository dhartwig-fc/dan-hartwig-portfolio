Guiding principles

Metadata first Analytics outputs are standardised into reusable metadata before they become intelligence products.
Intelligence manufacturing The platform converts financial crime complexity into repeatable, operational intelligence outputs.
Validation by design Governance, portability and regression controls are built into the production pathway.
Evidence led Outputs are structured around evidence, risk, confidence, rationale and investigation actions.
Enterprise scale The architecture is designed for multiple financial crime domains rather than isolated point solutions.
Private IP protected Public pages show capability and architecture while proprietary analytics and implementation remain private.

NEXUS is moving from prototype into operating platform.

The immediate delivery focus is runtime manufacturing and the TBML investigation layer demo. The long-term destination is a reusable financial crime intelligence operating platform that connects analytics, metadata, intelligence products, investigation workflows and decision support.
