Capability flow

01 Threat & Typology

Financial crime scenarios, typologies and intelligence requirements enter the platform.

02 Analytics Capability

Private analytics execute detection, scoring, network analysis and evidence generation.

03 Metadata Layer

Standardised metadata captures risk, confidence, classification, drivers and narrative.

04 Pattern Factory

Reusable rendering engine manufactures consistent intelligence products.

05 Investigation Workspace

Graph visualisation, intelligence boxes, evidence, actions and decision support.

06 Operational Outcomes

Investigations, risk prioritisation, operational action and executive reporting.
